常用I2C设备的地址

避免同一总线上地址冲突, 持续更新。

型号 名称 地址
PCA9536 4bits I2C GPIO 0x82/0x83
OV5640 OV CMOS Sensor 0x60/0x61
OV8825 OV CMOS Sensor 0x6C/0x6D
ES8316 audio codec 0x22/0x23
BU9873 RTC 0x64/0x65
24AA025E48 Serial EEPROMs with EUI-48™ Node Identity 0xA0/0xA1
EEPROM Standard EEPROM 0xA0~0xAF
ATSHA204A Secure authentication and validation device 0xC8/0xC9
PCF8591 8-bit A/D and D/A converter 0x90~0x9F
